About the Forests

Forests are vital ecosystems that regulate climate, purify air and water, and provide habitat for countless species. Our mission is to document biodiversity and support conservation projects that protect habitats and communities.

Featured Species

Asian Elephant

A keystone species that maintains forest structure. Threats include habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict.

Malayan Tiger

A top predator vital for ecosystem balance. Poaching and fragmentation endanger its populations.

Hornbill

Large frugivores essential for seed dispersal; their nesting trees are conservation priorities.
